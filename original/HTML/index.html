Monterey Bay Aquarirum (MBA): Fish or Foe game


You will be presented with an image of a fish and given its details, then will have to guess whether it is the best choice to buy, a certified food to buy by the Seafoodwatch website, whether it is a good alternative to buy, or a food to be avoided. You will then be given more information about that fish, and your score will be given to you at the end.


It is important to buy our seafood responsibly because it is crucial for protecting marine ecosystems, supporting sustainable fishing practices, and ensuring food security for current and future generations. It helps prevent overfishing, habitat destruction, and the decline of fish populations, while also promoting fair labor practices within the seafood industry.
